哲学 / Philosophy

Rooted in sensation,
shaped by culture.

Four years across luxury cultural institutions, beauty & cosmetics brands, and music identities have given me one conviction: the most powerful design is felt before it's read. Influenced by the world-building of Miyazaki, the surrealist tension of Magritte & Dalí, the obsessive pattern of Kusama, and the poetic graphic tradition of Kiyochika — I approach every project as the construction of an atmosphere, not just a visual.
